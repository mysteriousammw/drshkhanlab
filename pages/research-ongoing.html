Cardiovascular Regeneration Research

Cardiac Tissue Engineering and Regeneration

Investigating stem cell-based approaches for cardiac muscle regeneration. This research focuses on identifying molecular markers that regulate cardiomyocyte differentiation and developing biomaterial scaffolds for heart tissue repair.

Regenerative Medicine Stem Cells
2025-2027

Molecular Mechanisms of Cardiac Fibrosis

Studying the genetic and epigenetic regulation of cardiac fibroblast activation and extracellular matrix remodeling in heart disease. Targeting TGF-beta signaling pathways for therapeutic intervention.

Bioinformatics Research

2024-2026

Genomic Data Analysis for Livestock Improvement

Student Project: Developing computational pipelines for analyzing whole-genome sequencing data of indigenous cattle and sheep breeds. Focus on identifying selective sweeps and candidate genes for economically important traits.

Protein Structure Prediction and Drug Design

Microbial Biotechnology Research

2024-2026

Rumen Microbiome and Methane Reduction

Student Project: Characterizing the rumen microbial community structure in indigenous cattle and buffalo. Identifying microbial strains and dietary interventions that reduce enteric methane emissions while improving feed efficiency.

Microbiome Climate Smart
2025-2027

Antimicrobial Resistance in Livestock Pathogens

Student Project: Surveillance of antibiotic resistance genes in bacterial pathogens isolated from livestock. Molecular characterization of resistance mechanisms and alternative therapeutic strategies using bacteriophages.

AMR One Health
Animal Genetics

Livestock Genetic Improvement

2024-2026

Heat Tolerance in Indigenous Cattle

Identifying genetic markers and candidate genes associated with heat stress tolerance in Sahiwal, Red Sindhi, and Tharparkar cattle breeds. Utilizing GWAS and transcriptomics approaches.

Molecular Genetics Climate Adaptation
2023-2025

Embryo Biotechnology for Buffalo

Optimizing in vitro embryo production protocols for Nili-Ravi buffalo. Developing efficient oocyte maturation media and embryo culture systems for enhanced reproductive efficiency.

Embryo Biotech Reproduction
2025-2027

Kari Sheep Genetic Conservation

Building on our published work identifying DNA markers for gestation length, this project focuses on conserving the unique short-gestation Kari sheep subtype through marker-assisted breeding programs.
