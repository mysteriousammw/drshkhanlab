Ongoing Research

Active research projects advancing animal biotechnology

2024-2026

Climate-Resilient Livestock Breeding

Developing genetic strategies for heat tolerance and adaptability in indigenous cattle breeds of Pakistan. This project aims to identify key genes associated with heat stress resistance.

Molecular Genetics Funded Project
2023-2025

Advanced Embryo Technologies

Optimizing in vitro embryo production protocols for Nili-Ravi buffalo and indigenous goat breeds to enhance reproductive efficiency and conserve precious genetic resources.

Embryo Biotech Conservation
2024-2025

Disease Resistance Markers

Identification and validation of molecular markers for major livestock diseases including foot-and-mouth disease and mastitis to enable selective breeding for disease resistance.

Disease Genetics Markers
2025-2027

Indigenous Breed Conservation

Comprehensive genetic characterization and conservation strategies for endangered Pakistani goat breeds including Pahari, Jakar, and others to preserve biodiversity.

Conservation Genomics
2025-2026

Milk Production Genomics

Genome-wide association studies to identify genetic variants influencing milk yield, composition, and quality in dairy cattle for genomic selection programs.

GWAS Dairy
2024-2026

Cryopreservation Protocols

Development of improved sperm and embryo cryopreservation techniques for wildlife species and endangered livestock to establish genetic resource banks.
