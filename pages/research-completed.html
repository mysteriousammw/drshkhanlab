Completed Research

Past research projects and achievements

Disease Genetics

Disease Resistance Research

2020-2024

Foot-and-Mouth Disease Resistance Markers

Identification of genetic variants associated with FMD resistance in cattle through genome-wide association studies. Candidate genes in the MHC region and innate immunity pathways were characterized.

GWAS Immunogenetics View Publications
2019-2023

Mastitis Resistance in Dairy Cattle

Genetic analysis of mastitis resistance traits in Sahiwal and crossbred cattle. Identification of SNP markers in toll-like receptor genes and cytokine pathways associated with reduced somatic cell count.

Molecular Markers Dairy Health View Publications
Fiber Production

Wool Quality & Fiber Traits

2018-2022

Genetic Basis of Wool Quality in Pakistani Sheep

Molecular characterization of keratin-associated protein (KAP) genes influencing wool fiber diameter and staple length in Kachi, Baluchi, and Kajli sheep breeds. Marker-trait associations for breeding selection.

Candidate Genes Fiber Quality View Publications
2017-2021

Cashmere Production Gene Expression

Transcriptomic analysis of skin follicles during cashmere growth cycles in Changthangi goats. Identification of genes regulating secondary follicle development and fiber production.

Transcriptomics Gene Expression View Publications
Reproduction

Reproductive Biotechnology

2018-2022

IVF Protocol Optimization for Buffaloes

Development and optimization of ultrasound-guided oocyte retrieval and in vitro embryo production protocols for Nili-Ravi buffalo. Achieved improved blastocyst rates through optimized culture media.

IVF Buffalo View Publications
2017-2021

Embryo Sex Determination in Goats

Development of PCR-based molecular methods for pre-implantation embryo sex determination in Beetal and Nachi goats. Amplification of Y-chromosome specific sequences from single embryo biopsies.

PCR Embryo Biotech View Publications
Population Genetics

Genetic Diversity & Conservation

2019-2023

Genetic Characterization of Pakistani Cattle Breeds

Comprehensive microsatellite and mitochondrial DNA analysis of indigenous cattle populations including Sahiwal, Red Sindhi, and Tharparkar. Assessment of genetic diversity and population structure for conservation planning.

Microsatellites mtDNA View Publications
2017-2020

Kari Sheep Gestation Length Diversity

Discovery and characterization of the unique short-gestation (100 days) Kari sheep subtype from Chitral. Microsatellite analysis revealed distinct genetic clustering - published in Animals journal 2022.
