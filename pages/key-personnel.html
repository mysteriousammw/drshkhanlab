drshkhan@aup.edu.pk

Dr. Sher Hayat Khan is an Associate Professor at the Institute of Biotechnology and Genetic Engineering, University of Agriculture Peshawar. He specializes in molecular genetics, reproductive biotechnology, and genetic improvement of livestock. His research focuses on gene identification and characterization, embryo biotechnology, conservation genetics, and developing innovative solutions for challenges in animal agriculture and biodiversity conservation in Pakistan.

Research Interests

Molecular Genetics

Gene identification, characterization, and utilization for genetic improvement of livestock breeds.

Neurodegenerative Diseases

Research on molecular mechanisms, biomarkers, and therapeutic strategies for disorders such as Alzheimer's, Prion's and Parkinson's disease..

Genetic Engineering

Developing genetic strategies for enhanced productivity and disease resistance.

Animal Biotechnology

Use of animal models for disease research, regenerative medicine, and biotechnological advancements.
