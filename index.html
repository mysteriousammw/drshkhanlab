About Us

Advancing Science Through Innovation

The Animal Biotechnology Laboratory at the University of Agriculture Peshawar leads cutting-edge research in molecular genetics, reproductive biotechnology, and genetic improvement of livestock.

Our multidisciplinary team combines molecular biology, genomics, and reproductive technologies to address critical challenges in human, animal agriculture and conservation.

Molecular Genetics

Gene identification, characterization, and utilization for genetic improvement

Reproductive Biotechnology

Advanced techniques in embryo transfer, IVF, and cryopreservation

Genetic Engineering

Developing transgenic animals for research and agricultural applications
